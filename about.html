  • About Books4Hoos

    How often have you been disappointed with a textbook price? How often are you given less than the book is worth, only to watch it be resold to other students at a higher price? Enough is enough. A service by students, for students. Our aim is to help current UVA students get the most bang for their buck. Got a book to sell? Upload it to our site and we'll help you find buyers that match your desired price. Got a book to buy? Browse our online catalog and find the best deals on high-demand used textbooks! All our books are double checked for top quality, so you can rest assured that your books will be in great condition.

    Does this site make money for the creators?

    No. This site is completely non-profit and under maintenance by its developers, Peter Bahng, Alexander Kim, and Max Serpe.
